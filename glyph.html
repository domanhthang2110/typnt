The history of typography begins with the paired punch and counter punch system invented by Francesco Griffo under the patronage of Aldus Manutius in 1494. The mechanical system of movable type and printing press invented by Johannes Gutenberg flourished, spreading to become the standard across Europe. Various foundries competed in the type design scene, developing new forms to fit their needs.

18px

Digital typography has transformed as technology advances. From bitmap fonts to scalable vector graphics, the art of type design continues to evolve. With web fonts and variable font technology, typography becomes increasingly dynamic, responsive, and customizable across different devices and platforms, enabling designers to create more sophisticated typographic experiences than ever before.
